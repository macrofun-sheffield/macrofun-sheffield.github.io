Today we had Gabi Putnoki from the [GNRR](https://www.graphicnovelreadingroom.com/) that talked us about her editorial work on making research outreach comics.

She has  succesfully already edited several comics in different departments accross the university to advertise the work of researchers in a easily accessible way.
The process entails working closely with the artist (drawing) and the research team to develop a scenario and illustrate it.
This results in cool and easily shareable small comics (or zines) that illustrate some aspect of the research team's work.

You can find the slides [here](https://macrofun-sheffield.github.io/images/UoS-Biosciences_demo.pdf) along with much more detailed information.
